Welcome to the Decentralized Identity Platform

Decentralized identity platform (DIP) for secure storage and easy access to personal documents using IPFS,Filecoin & Web3Storage.js .

Our platform provides a secure and decentralized way to manage and store your personal documents and information. You can easily add, view and manage your documents using our user-friendly interface.
